From conceptual design to building the plane large number of tasks are completed by the aerodynamics subsystem. Aerodynamics and manufacturing subsystem can be further subdivided into the following groups:

Aerodynamics: All the CFD analysis required to meet the given performance requirements is carried out.

Structures: CAD modeling and structural analysis of the plane body is done in this group.

Payload: Designing the payload deployment mechanism for the precise delivery of the payload.

UGV: Development of an unmanned ground vehicle to be deployed as a payload to carry the necessary goods to the exact location on the map.
